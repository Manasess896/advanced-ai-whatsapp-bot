Privacy at a Glance

Here’s a quick summary of our data practices. For full details, please read the entire policy.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how our WhatsApp Bot ("the Bot") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you interact with it. By using the Bot, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. Our goal is to be transparent about how we handle your data to provide you with an intelligent and helpful service.

2. Information We Collect

To provide a seamless conversational experience, we collect the following types of information:

Information You Provide:
  • Message Content: The text of all messages you send to the Bot. This is essential for the AI to understand your requests and generate relevant replies.
  • WhatsApp Information: Your WhatsApp phone number and display name (if public). This is used to identify you as a unique user and maintain a persistent conversation history.
Information Collected Automatically:
  • Conversation Metadata: Timestamps for when messages are sent, delivered, and read. This helps us troubleshoot and monitor service health.
  • Technical Logs: Anonymous data about response times and errors. This is used purely for performance monitoring and service improvement.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to operate and improve the Bot. Here’s how:

  • To Provide Conversation Context: We store your message history to ensure the Bot remembers previous parts of the conversation, allowing for more natural and relevant follow-up responses.
  • For AI Processing: Your messages are sent to the Groq AI API, which uses LLaMA models to analyze the text and generate an intelligent, human-like reply.
  • To Deliver the Service: We use your WhatsApp number to send the AI-generated responses back to you via the WhatsApp Cloud API (Meta).
  • For Analytics and Improvement: We analyze aggregated, anonymous data to understand usage patterns, identify common issues, and improve the Bot's performance and reliability.
  • To Ensure Legal Compliance: We may use your information to comply with applicable laws, enforce our terms, and protect the rights and safety of our users.

4. Data Retention

  • Conversation History: Your conversation data is stored indefinitely in our secure MongoDB database to maintain a long-term memory for your interactions, unless you request its deletion.
  • Context Memory Limit: For generating immediate responses, the Bot's AI considers only the last 20 messages. Older messages are kept in the database for your record but are not actively used for AI context.
  • System Logs: Technical logs are retained for up to 30 days for debugging and performance analysis.
  • Deletion Requests: You have the right to request the complete and permanent deletion of your data. See Section 7 for instructions.

5. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. However, to function, the Bot relies on the following trusted third-party services that process your data on our behalf:

  • Groq AI API: Your messages are sent to Groq for AI response generation. Groq Privacy Policy
  • Meta (WhatsApp Cloud API): Handles message delivery between you and the Bot. WhatsApp Privacy Policy
  • MongoDB Atlas: Provides the secure cloud database where your conversation history is stored. MongoDB Privacy Policy

Legal Disclosure: We may disclose your information if required by law, such as in response to a court order or other legal process, or to protect our rights and safety.

6. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement the following measures:

  • All data is encrypted in transit (using TLS/SSL) and at rest.
  • Access to the database is strictly limited to authorized systems and personnel.
  • We follow industry best practices for application security to prevent unauthorized access.

While we implement robust security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your data to the best of our ability.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:
